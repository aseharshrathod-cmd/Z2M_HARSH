About Steve Jobs

Steven Paul Jobs (1955–2011) was an American entrepreneur, inventor, and business leader. He co-founded Apple Inc. and played a key role in revolutionizing personal computing, music, phones, and animated movies.

Major Achievements

1976 – Apple Founded

Co-founded Apple with Steve Wozniak and Ronald Wayne in a garage.

1984 – Macintosh

Introduced the Macintosh, the first mass-market personal computer with a GUI.

1986 – Pixar

Purchased Pixar and helped turn it into a legendary animation studio.

2001 – iPod

Revolutionized the music industry with the iPod and iTunes.

2007 – iPhone

Redefined smartphones and changed the tech world forever.

2010 – iPad

Created a new category of consumer electronics with the iPad.
